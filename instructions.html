How To Play

1. Game Objective

Find all matching pairs of cards in as few moves as possible. Test your memory and try to remember where each character is located.

2. Game Rules

Click on any card to flip it over and reveal the character. Then try to find its matching pair. If you find a match, the cards stay flipped. If not, they turn back over.

3. Game Controls

Use your mouse or touch screen to click on cards to flip them. You can only flip two cards at a time.

4. Winning

The game is complete when all pairs are matched. Try to complete the game with as few moves as possible!
